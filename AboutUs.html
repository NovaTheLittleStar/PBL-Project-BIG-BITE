SERVED EVERY DAY SINCE 1990

Big bite opened on Thanksgiving Day 1990. Chef / Owner Ho Mei Chu began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and her neighbours out a small kitchen at the corner of Hudson and North Moore St. in Tribeca. Today,NYC's beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 27 years of classic, made from scartch American cooking.
